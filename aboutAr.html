MARAKUMA NEJAM

   A. R. Rahman is an Indian composer, singer-songwriter, music producer, musician, multi-instrumentalist and
   philanthropist.
   Described as the world's most prominent and prolific film composer by Time,his works are notable for
   integrating Eastern classical music with electronic music sounds, world music genres and traditional orchestral
   arrangements.
   He has won two Academy Awards,two Grammy Awards,BAFTA Award,a Golden Globe,four National Film Awards,
   fifteen Filmfare Awards and thirteen Filmfare Awards South in addition to numerous other awards and nominations.
   His extensive body of work for film and the stage earned him the nickname "the Mozart of Madras" 
   and several Tamil commentators and fans have coined him the nickname Isai Puya

INDIAN ARTISTS

 
    Music refers to the diverse musical traditions and styles that originate from the Indian subcontinent.
    It is one of the oldest and most intricate musical systems in the world, with a rich history that dates back thousands of years.
    Indian music encompasses various genres, including classical, folk, devotional, film music, and popular music. 
    Folk music in India varies greatly from region to region and reflects the cultural diversity of the country.
    Each state and community has its distinct folk music traditions, instruments, and dance forms. 
    These folk songs often depict daily life, love, nature, and celebrate various festivals and occasions.
